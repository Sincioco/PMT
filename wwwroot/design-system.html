Dialog behavior

Dialogs use an opaque-enough elevated surface, a clear border, contained scrolling, and visible actions.

Glass surface

Use it sparingly

Navigation, menus, and bounded overlays may use glass. Data-heavy table cells and long-form reading surfaces should not.
